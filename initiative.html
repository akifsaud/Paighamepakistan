Our Initiative

Education First

Scholarship Program

Comprehensive educational support for children of convicted prisoners in Sindh's jails — breaking the cycle of crime through knowledge.

Breaking the Cycle Through Education

The scholarship program is the cornerstone of Paigham-e-Pakistan. It provides comprehensive educational support to children whose parents are convicted prisoners in Sindh's jails, ensuring that a parent's incarceration does not determine a child's future.

Full Tuition Coverage — Complete school fee payments for primary and secondary education

Learning Materials — Textbooks, notebooks, stationery, and all educational supplies

School Uniforms — Complete uniform sets to ensure students attend school with dignity

Personal Mentorship — Dedicated mentors for academic guidance and emotional support

Career Counseling — Professional guidance to help students plan their futures

Education for Every Child

Complete Support

Tuition, books, uniforms, and all educational materials covered.

Mentorship

Personal academic mentors and career counselors for each student.

Family Support

Holistic family support ensuring a stable environment for learning.

Progress Tracking

Regular monitoring and evaluation of academic progress and wellbeing.

Transforming Lives

Rehabilitation Framework

A comprehensive approach to psychological and social rehabilitation for inmates and their families.

Psychological Support

Professional counseling services for inmates addressing trauma, mental health, and emotional wellbeing as foundations for successful rehabilitation.

Social Reintegration

Structured programs preparing inmates for successful reentry into society, including life skills training, vocational education, and community connection building.

Family Reconnection

Programs that strengthen family bonds during and after incarceration, supporting the emotional needs of both prisoners and their family members.

Building Peace

Peacebuilding Efforts

De-radicalization and counter-extremism through education, dialogue, and community engagement.

Peace Through Understanding

The peacebuilding component of Paigham-e-Pakistan addresses the root causes of extremism and radicalization within prison populations. Through structured educational programs, interfaith dialogue, and critical thinking development, the initiative creates environments where peace can take root.

Counter-Extremism Education — Programs addressing radical ideologies through knowledge and critical thinking

Interfaith Harmony — Promoting tolerance and mutual respect among diverse prison populations

Community Awareness — Public education campaigns promoting peace and social cohesion

Peace & Harmony for All

How It Works

Implementation Strategy

A structured, multi-phase approach to program delivery across Sindh's correctional facilities.

Identification & Assessment

Working with prison authorities to identify eligible children and assess their educational needs and current status.

Enrollment & Support

Enrolling children in appropriate educational institutions and providing complete scholarship packages.

Mentorship & Monitoring

Assigning personal mentors and conducting regular progress assessments to ensure academic success.

Evaluation & Scaling

Measuring outcomes, documenting best practices, and expanding the program to additional facilities.

Multi-Ministry Collaboration

The implementation relies on seamless coordination between three key government departments:

Ministry of School Education & Literacy — manages school enrollment and academic standards

Ministry of Prisons Sindh — facilitates prison-level access and family coordination

Ministry of Universities & Boards — supports higher education pathways
