Grand Launch

Launching Ceremony

The official launch of the scholarship program at Central Jail Karachi.

Central Jail Karachi — A Historic Moment

The Paigham-e-Pakistan scholarship program was officially launched at Central Jail Karachi in a landmark ceremony attended by senior government officials, ministry representatives, prison authorities, and educational leaders.

The ceremony marked a historic first — the formal commitment by government ministries to collaborate on providing educational support specifically to children of convicted prisoners, recognizing their right to education regardless of their parents' circumstances.

Location: Central Jail Karachi, Sindh

Attendees: Government officials, ministry representatives, prison authorities, educators

Significance: Official government endorsement of the scholarship program

Capacity Building

Workshops & Seminars

Prison-wise reform workshops conducted across Sindh's correctional facilities.

Educational Reform Workshops

Workshops focused on integrating educational programs within prison facilities, training prison staff in educational support, and developing curriculum adapted to the needs of inmates and their children.

De-radicalization Seminars

Specialized seminars addressing extremist ideologies through education, critical thinking, and interfaith dialogue. These programs engage both inmates and prison staff in building resilience against radicalization.

Community Engagement Sessions

Public awareness and participation activities engaging local communities in supporting the rehabilitation and reintegration of prisoners' families, building a network of social support.

Making a Difference

Scholarship Distributions

Events where educational support packages are distributed to eligible children.

Phase 1

Central Jail Karachi Distribution

The first major scholarship distribution event, providing educational packages including tuition support, textbooks, and uniforms to children of inmates at Central Jail Karachi.

Phase 2

Expansion to District Jails

Extension of the scholarship program to district jail facilities across Sindh, reaching more children and families in need of educational support.

Phase 3

Province-wide Implementation

Full-scale implementation across all correctional facilities in Sindh, establishing a comprehensive network of educational support for prisoners' children.

Ongoing

Continuous Support & Monitoring

Ongoing scholarship renewals, progress monitoring, mentorship check-ins, and program improvements based on feedback and outcomes assessment.
