Inspirational Words

Messages of Hope

Words from national leaders and dignitaries that inspire and guide the mission of Paigham-e-Pakistan.

Home / Messages
Father of the Nation

Quaid-e-Azam Muhammad Ali Jinnah

"The aim of education is not only to enable a person to earn his living but to make him a worthy citizen of the state."

Quaid-e-Azam Muhammad Ali Jinnah

Founder of Pakistan (1876 – 1948)

The words of the Quaid-e-Azam form the philosophical foundation of the Paigham-e-Pakistan initiative. His vision of education as a tool for nation-building and citizenship — not merely economic advancement — resonates profoundly with our mission.

When we educate children of convicted prisoners, we are fulfilling the Quaid's vision: creating worthy citizens who contribute positively to the state, regardless of the circumstances of their birth.

Armed Forces Leadership

Field Marshal Syed Asim Munir

"Peace and education are inseparable pillars of a strong nation. When we invest in education, we invest in lasting peace. The children we nurture today will be the guardians of Pakistan's security and prosperity tomorrow."

Field Marshal Syed Asim Munir

Message on Peace & Education

The endorsement from Pakistan's military leadership underscores the national security dimension of educational rehabilitation. Breaking the cycle of crime and extremism through education is recognized as a vital component of Pakistan's overall security strategy.

The support for Paigham-e-Pakistan at the highest levels of military leadership reflects the understanding that true security comes not only from defense, but from building an educated, empowered citizenry.
