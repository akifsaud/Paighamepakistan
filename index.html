Our Focus

Key Focus Areas

Paigham-e-Pakistan addresses multiple dimensions of human rights, peace, and social rehabilitation through a comprehensive, multi-stakeholder approach.

Education & Scholarships

Comprehensive scholarship program providing tuition, books, uniforms, and mentorship for children of convicted prisoners in Sindh.

Peace & De-radicalization

Strategic peacebuilding and counter-extremism initiatives aimed at creating harmony and preventing the spread of radical ideologies.

Rehabilitation & Reform

Psychological and social rehabilitation frameworks for prisoners and their families, supporting successful reintegration into society.

Human Dignity & Justice

Advocating for compassion-based justice that upholds human dignity, protecting the rights of vulnerable children and families.

Educational Transformation

Leveraging education as the most powerful tool for social change, breaking intergenerational cycles of crime and poverty.

International Collaboration

Building partnerships with global human rights bodies and international organizations to amplify impact and share best practices.

About the Initiative

A Vision of Paigham-e-Pakistan

Paigham-e-Pakistan stands as the world's first revolutionary human rights and peace initiative, born from the conviction that education is the most powerful weapon against the cycle of crime and extremism.

In collaboration with the Ministry of School Education and Literacy Department, Ministry of Prisons Sindh, and Ministry of Universities & Boards, the initiative provides comprehensive educational support to children whose parents are incarcerated in Sindh's jails.

Government-endorsed national-level program

Multi-ministry collaboration across education and justice sectors

Comprehensive scholarship covering tuition, materials, and mentoring
